Habit Tracker

This application was inspired by a variety of books on habits. The latest of which Power of Habit Book Summary by Charles Duhigg drove home the point necessary to have a good habit: cue, routine, reward. So often I've missed the reward. Without a reward you're just using self control. Self control is not a good long term strategy.

Unfortunatly, this app does nothing for a reward. But at least you can track and download so you can play with your statistics.
